Welcome to Money Pot

The peer to peer social lending platform.

Create your pots and invite others!

Create your own fundraising pots and invite others to contribute. Easily set a goal and track your progress. Share your pot with friends and family to raise money for whatever you need. Whether it's a special occasion or a personal project, our platform makes it easy to reach your financial goals.

Track your pots!

Keep track of all your fundraising pots in one place. Monitor contributions, set reminders, and see how close you are to reaching your goals. Our user-friendly interface allows you to easily manage and update your pots, so you can focus on reaching your financial goals.

Join other pots!

Join pots created by others and contribute to their fundraising goals. Browse a variety of pots and choose the ones that align with your values. Support friends, family, or causes you care about by joining their pots and making a difference.
